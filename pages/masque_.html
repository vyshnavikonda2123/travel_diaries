Masque

Masque is an acclaimed, high-end, modern Indian fine-dining restaurant in Mumbai, ranked #19 on Asia's 50 Best Restaurants 2025. Located in a renovated cotton mill in Mahalaxmi, it specializes in a seasonal, ingredient-driven, ten-course tasting menu curated by Chef Varun Totlani. It is known for innovative, sustainable, and sophisticated cuisine.Key Details:Location: Unit G3, Laxmi Woollen Mills, Shakti Mills Lane, Mahalaxmi, Mumbai.Concept: Focus on locally sourced, foraged ingredients and reimagined traditional Indian cooking.Menu: Primarily a 10-course tasting menu.Dining Spaces: Includes the main dining room, 'The Lab' for intimate culinary experiences, and 'The Living Room' for cocktails.Hours: Open for dinner Tuesday-Sunday; Sunday lunch available. Closed on Mondays.Reservation: Highly recommended, given its reputation as a leading destination.Highlights:Accolades: Multiple inclusions on Asia's 50 Best Restaurants list.Experience: Known for a romantic, sophisticated, and, for many, an expensive fine-dining experience.Bar: Features creative,, and customized cocktail experiences.It is recommended to book through the Masque official website or contact them directly.
