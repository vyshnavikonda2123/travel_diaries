Gufha

Gufha, located at Hotel Pai Vista on Bangalore-Nilgiri Road, is a popular cave-themed restaurant in Mysore known for its unique, dark rustic ambiance and waiter attire resembling wildlife officers. It serves North Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines, generally opening for lunch at 12 PM.Key Details:Location: 35/A, Bangalore-Nilgiri Road, Hotel Pai Vista, Mysore.Theme: Cave-themed, offering a unique, adventurous dining atmosphere.Cuisine: Multi-cuisine, specializing in North Indian dishes, with highchairs available.Specialties: Gufha special platter, butter chicken, and cream of chicken soup are highly recommended.Cost: Approximately ₹1050 for two people.Amenities: Offers dine-in, full bar service, and parking.It is highly recommended for families and visitors seeking a thematic, fun experience, especially noted for being child-friendly. Booking a reservation in advance is advisable.
