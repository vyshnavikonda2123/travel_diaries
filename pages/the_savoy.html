The Savoy

Hotel Savoy Kansas City is a historic and culturally significant hotel located in downtown Kansas City, Missouri. Originally opened in 1888, it is one of the oldest continuously operating hotels west of the Mississippi River. The hotel has been beautifully restored to preserve its rich heritage while offering modern comforts to guests. The architecture of the Savoy reflects classic 19th-century design, with elegant details and a timeless atmosphere. Its interiors combine historic charm with contemporary style, creating a unique and inviting environment. One of the highlights of the hotel is the Savoy Restaurant, which is among the oldest restaurants in the United States and is known for its refined American cuisine. The dining experience reflects both tradition and innovation. The hotel also features a museum that showcases artifacts and exhibits related to Kansas City’s history, adding an educational and cultural dimension to the stay. Guests can explore the stories of the past while enjoying modern hospitality. The rooms are designed to provide comfort and style, blending vintage aesthetics with updated amenities. Each space reflects the hotel’s historic character while ensuring a pleasant stay. Its central location makes it convenient for visitors to explore nearby attractions, entertainment districts, and cultural landmarks. The hotel attracts history enthusiasts, tourists, and business travelers alike. The Savoy is known for its warm service and welcoming atmosphere, offering guests a memorable and authentic experience. It stands as a symbol of Kansas City’s rich past and evolving present. Overall, Hotel Savoy Kansas City offers a unique blend of history, culture, and comfort, making it a distinctive destination for travelers seeking more than just a place to stay.
