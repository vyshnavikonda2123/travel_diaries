Jamavar

Jamavar is a renowned fine-dining Indian restaurant brand famous for authentic Northern and pan-Indian cuisine, founded by Dinesh Nair in 2001. Known for opulent decor, including gold-leaf paintings, it operates within The Leela Palaces (Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, etc.) and has a Michelin-starred location in Mayfair, London.Key Info & Locations:Signature Dishes: Raan-e-Jamavar, Murgh Makhani, Dal-e-Jamavar, and Galouti Kebabs.Ambience: Traditional Indian luxury featuring hand-carved screens, silver table settings, and rich textiles.Locations:London (Mayfair): Located at 8 Mount Street.Bengaluru: Located at The Leela Palace, known for a regal setting.Chennai: Located at The Leela Palace, offers "Jashn-e-Jamavar" Sunday brunch.Mumbai: Located at The Leela Mumbai.Dining Style: Fine dining, often following a "smart casual" dress code.Signature Experience:The brand embodies the philosophy of "Atithi Devo Bhava" (Guest is God) and was initially created to bring refined North Indian cuisine to Southern India, later expanding globally. The London branch has been recognized with a Michelin star.
