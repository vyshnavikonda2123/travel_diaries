Oberoi Udaivilas

The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, is an ultra-luxury palace hotel situated on 30 acres of former hunting grounds along Lake Pichola. Designed to emulate a traditional Rajasthani palace with domes, courtyards, and gardens, it offers top-tier amenities including 87 rooms (some with private pools), nine swimming pools, a spa, and exceptional personalized butler service.Key Highlights:Location: Situated on the banks of Lake Pichola, offering stunning views of the City Palace, with access often via a private boat ride.Architecture: Designed in Mewari style with hand-carved stone columns, murals, and expansive, manicured gardens.Accommodations: Features luxurious rooms, including Premier Rooms with private courtyards and Suites with private pools.Dining: Renowned restaurants offering international and traditional Indian cuisine, including private lakeside dining options.Recognition: Frequently recognized as one of the best resorts in Asia and the world by Travel + Leisure.Key Amenities & Services:Spa & Wellness: A full-service spa offering Ayurvedic and Western treatments, plus yoga sessions.Butler Service: Dedicated 24-hour butler service for all guests.Activities: Complimentary boat rides, heritage walks, and cooking sessions with chefs.Accessibility: Located roughly 45 minutes from Udaipur Airport (UDR).
