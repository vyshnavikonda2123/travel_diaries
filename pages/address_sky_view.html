Address Sky View

The Address Residence Sky View est un ensemble de gratte-ciel en construction à Dubaï aux Émirats arabes unis. Elles s'élèveront à 261 et 237 mètres. Elles abriteront des résidences ainsi qu'un hôtel pour la plus petite. Leur achèvement est prévu pour 2018. Address Sky View is a luxury 5-star hotel in Downtown Dubai, featuring two towers connected by a bridge, located on Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashed Boulevard. It is renowned for its rooftop infinity pool with panoramic views of the Burj Khalifa and direct, air-conditioned access to the Dubai Mall/metro station.Key DetailsLocation: Downtown Dubai, near Burj Khalifa.Address: Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashed Boulevard, Downtown Dubai, PO Box 111969, Dubai, UAE.Amenities: Infinity pool on the 54th floor, spa, gym, kids' club, and multiple dining options, including Ce La Vi.Accommodation: Luxurious rooms and suites, including Premier Rooms, featuring contemporary design and often direct Burj Khalifa views.Unique Features: The Sky Views Observatory with a glass slide and walk is located on-site.
